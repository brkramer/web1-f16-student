Personal Portfolio Process Page

Goals

This portfolio website will be linked to job and internship applications. It will serve as an additional and more personalized collection of my work, in addition to my Behance page. I want it to show my strengths in design, as well as my other art-related interests. The content will be curated to show the work I am most proud of, and best represents my interests as a designer. I want to show that I can be versatile and have a wide range of interests, in order to add depth and personality to my portfolio.
